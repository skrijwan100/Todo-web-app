About My To-Do App

Welcome to My To-Do App, a simple yet effective tool designed to help you manage your daily tasks and stay organized. Whether you're planning your day, organizing a project, or simply keeping track of your personal goals, My To-Do App offers an intuitive interface to ensure that nothing falls through the cracks.

This application was built using Flask, a lightweight and versatile web framework for Python. The back-end is powered by SQLite, a fast and reliable database, ensuring that your tasks are securely stored and easily accessible.

Features of My To-Do App include:

  • Add new tasks with ease
  • Update existing tasks to keep your list up to date
  • Delete tasks that are no longer needed
  • View all your tasks at a glance

We hope My To-Do App helps you stay productive and organized. If you have any feedback or suggestions for improvement, feel free to reach out. Thank you for choosing My To-Do App to manage your tasks!
